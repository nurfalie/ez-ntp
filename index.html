EzNTP

About EzNTP

    Oh! do not attack me with your watch. A watch is always too fast or too slow. I cannot be dictated to by a watch.
      - Jane Austen
EzNTP is a simple and elegant set of programs that provide clock synchronization between two computers. The programs do not require configuration or maintenance. The daemon is multi-threaded, and communications between the hosts are TCP/IP-based. The client software lacks sophistication as it emphasizes accuracy.

Obtaining EzNTP

EzNTP has been tested successfully on a variety of operating systems, including Alpha FreeBSD 4.11, Alpha FreeBSD 5.4, CentOS 4.3, Debian Sparc64 Linux 4.0_r1, FreeBSD 5.4, FreeBSD 5.5, FreeBSD 6.2, Gentoo Sparc64 Linux, Mac OS 10.3.9, Solaris 9, and Solaris 10.
