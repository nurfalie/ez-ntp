EzNTP

About EzNTP

    Oh! do not attack me with your watch. A watch is always too fast or too slow. I cannot be dictated to by a watch.
      - Jane Austen
EzNTP is a set of simple programs that provide clock synchronization between two computers. The programs do not require configuration or maintenance. The daemon portion is multi-threaded. Communications between the hosts are TCP-based. The client software lacks sophistication as it emphasizes accuracy.
